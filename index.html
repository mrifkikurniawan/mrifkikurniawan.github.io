    Biography

    I am an artifical intelligence (AI) scientist at Nodeflux, a vision AI company in Indonesia, with practical hands-on experience developing deep learning-based computer vision for recognition and surveillance. I have worked on building object recognition for real-time vehicle surveillance by employing deep learning-based object detector. Recently, I am developing retail products/Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) modules including general detector and fine-grained classifier model. In order to resolve the intrinsic issues, I consider fine-grained and scale-aware classification task while training the model.

    Moreover, I am a master student at Computer Science and Technology, Xi’an Jiaotong University. Currently I am joining Machine Intelligence and Vision Group (MIV-XJTU) lead by Prof. Yihong Gong for solving fundamental issues on machine learning and computer vision especially studying and exploring continual learning and representation learning under supervision of Dr. Yang Qi and Dr. Xing Wei.

    Previously, I finished my bachelor in engineering physics at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember Surabaya with bachelor thesis on “Design of Mobile Predictor for Maritime Weather Forecasting Using Hybrid Method of Type 2 Fuzzy Logic and Artificial Neural Network with Differential Evolution Algorithm Optimization” under supervision of Dr. Ir. Syamsul Arifin M.T and Prof. Dr. Ir. Aulia Siti Aisjah M.T..
